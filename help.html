Welcome to Join, your Kanban project management tool guide. Learn what Join is, its benefits, and how to use it effectively.

What is Join?

Join is your go-to project management tool with a Kanban system, ideal for teamwork. Easily assign tasks, track progress, and manage your workflow efficiently.

How to use it

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use Join:

1. Create Tasks: Click "Create Task," fill in title and description, set category, date, and priority. Assign tasks to team members from your contacts.
2. Kanban Board: Access the "Board" to organize tasks with drag-and-drop into columns like "To Do," "In Progress," "Awaiting Feedback," and "Done."
3. Task Summary: The "Summary" provides an overview of all tasks, including progress and deadlines. Stay on top of urgent tasks and track completed work.

Enjoy the benefits of Join!
